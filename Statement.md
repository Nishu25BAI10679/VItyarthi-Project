Problem Statement:
Managing personal finances & expenses and sticking to their monthly budget can be challenging without proper tools. Many people overspend because they lack simple ways to track expenses and understand how much money they have left to spend. This often leads to financial stress.

Scope of the Project:
Budget Manager aims to offer an easy to use command-line program which helps users set and maintain a monthly budget. It focuses on budget tracking, expense management, and daily spending recommendations and alerts. The project uses basic file storage to keep data persistent across sessions without needing complex databases.

Target Users:
This tool is ideal for individuals looking for a straightforward budget tracker without a steep learning curve. It is suitable for beginners who want to improve their financial awareness through simple inputs and timely alerts. It also fits and fulfill conditions of anyone who prefers a lightweight, no-frills expense tracker on their computer.

High-Level Features:
1.Set a total monthly budget with user-friendly prompts.
2.Add detailed expenses categorized by purpose.
3.View a list of all recorded expenses .
4.Check the remaining budget anytime with daily spending limits advised.
5.Receive alerts when spending reaches half, low, or over-budget thresholds.
6.Persistent savings of data in a simple text file for continuity.
